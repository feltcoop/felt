Welcome to the Felt.dev consentful systems onboarding sketch!

This sketch is a proof of concept, side-by-side comparsion between two onboarding flows:

  • The left column uses dark design patterns that create an unconsentful experience.
  • The one on the right uses the affirmative consent design framework described by consentful.systems

The goal here is to compare and contrast the two design patterns. During the experience, ask yourself: which would you prefer?
